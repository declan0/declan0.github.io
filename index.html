About

I’m Declan O’Reilly. My interests include firm behavior, antitrust, and the ways companies shape everyday life. I graduated from Grinnell College with degrees in economics and music.

I now work in economic consulting, where I study firm behavior and apply econometric analysis to antitrust questions. I’m particularly interested in multimarket contact, tacit coordination, algorithmic pricing, and how technology in the hands of firms influences everyday life.
